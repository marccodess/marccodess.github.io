About Me

Since completing my postgraduate studies in 2017, I have established a career in Data Science and Advanced Analytics, accumulating experience across both innovative startups and enterprise organisations within logistics, eCommerce, and telecommunication domains.

My cross-industry experience has enabled me to develop comprehensive expertise in both technical implementation and business strategy. As an advanced Python developer, I specialise in developing and deploying sophisticated machine learning and artificial intelligence solutions, leveraging cutting-edge technologies to deliver measurable business value.

Currently, I lead a team of Data Scientists at Virgin Media O2, where we drive transformative innovation and develop high-impact solutions that revolutionise network operations and customer experience.

This portfolio functions as a technical repository of my professional expertise and open-source contributions. Originally initiated as an experimental development project during the COVID-19 pandemic, it has matured into a comprehensive technical platform that integrates my GitHub repositories, professional network connections, and technical publications.
